Precautions
The system-matched power supply box exclusive for GSK 983Ta. not
supply this power other devices (such magnetic
valve); otherwise, serious danger may occur!
Requirements for electrical cabinet
The electrical cabinet adopts full-enclosed structure and dustproof design. The temperature
difference between outside and inside the cabinet should less than 10°C; or, heat-exchange
system should installed. The ambient temperature should not exceed 45°C. Prevent the entry
dust, coolant and organic solution.
Grounding
The cabinet should protectively grounded, and the continuity should meet the requirement
GB5226.1-2002. Well grounding the essential condition for stable operation. The grounding
wires different parts cannot connected with each other series. The grounding strip whose
thickness not less than 3mm should installed the cabinet, the protective grounding terminals
should connected grounding strip with yellow-green wires independently, and when the
grounding strip connected with the ground, its grounding resistance should less than 40.
